## Full description (max 4000)
ruta puts the social sites you already use — X, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Reddit, Bluesky,
Threads, LinkedIn, Tumblr and Mastodon — into one clean, private Android app, with ads and
trackers blocked. No separate apps, no clutter, no tracking.

FEATURES

• All your feeds, one app — a simple grid of social sites; tap to open. Add any other site too.
• Ads and trackers blocked out of the box (EasyList + EasyPrivacy). Add your own filter lists.
• Multiple accounts, kept separate — each account gets its own isolated profile; switch between
  them from the bottom dock. Long-press to reorder or close.
• Force dark mode on any website — on by default.
• Cleaner links — strips utm_, fbclid and other tracking junk when you copy.
• Make it yours — per-site custom CSS, a built-in proxy, desktop-site toggle, and the option to
  hide the address bar.
• Genuinely private — no analytics, no accounts, no ads, and no third-party tracking SDKs. ruta
  never reports on you.
• Modern and beautiful — Material You design with dynamic color, dark/light, edge-to-edge.

ruta blocks ads and trackers on the sites you open and keeps your accounts isolated, but it does
not make those sites anonymous — they still apply their own privacy policies.

Open source: https://github.com/klppl/ruta
