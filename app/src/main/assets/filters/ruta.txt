! Title: ruta filters
! Description: Built-in filter list maintained in the ruta repo. Add network, cosmetic and
!   procedural (:has-text) rules here for granular control without depending on a remote list.
! Expires: 7 days
! Version: 1
!
! Syntax: standard Adblock Plus. ruta enforces:
!   - Network:    ||host^   and   @@||host^   (host-anchored only)   plus hostfile lines
!   - Cosmetic:   domain##selector            (plain element hiding)
!   - Procedural: domain##selector:has-text(/regex/flags)   (text-matched hiding, page-side)
! Unsupported (silently dropped): path/regex/wildcard network rules, scriptlets (#$#, +js),
!   and procedural operators other than :has-text / :contains.

! ============================================================================
! App-install / "open in app" interstitials
! ============================================================================
! Meta sites throw up a modal nagging you to open or install the native app. The modal uses
! obfuscated atomic class names and carries no stable id, so it can only be targeted reliably
! by its call-to-action text. Matching aria-modal dialogs by CTA leaves real dialogs (post
! composers, photo viewers, menus) untouched.
